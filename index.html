Loan Tips & Financial Guides

How EMI is Calculated

EMI is calculated using the loan amount, interest rate, and repayment period. A lower interest rate or longer tenure can reduce monthly payments.

Choosing the Right Loan

Before taking a loan, compare interest rates, processing fees, repayment flexibility, and lender reputation to find the most suitable option.

Reducing Loan Interest Costs

Making larger down payments, choosing shorter loan terms, and maintaining a good credit history can help reduce overall interest costs.

What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount paid every month to repay a loan. It includes both the principal amount and the interest charged by the lender.
