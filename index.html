Financial Tips & Guides

How Monthly Payments Are Calculated: Monthly payments use the loan amount, interest rate, and repayment period. A lower rate or longer tenure reduces monthly payments but increases total interest.

Power of SIP Investing: Systematic Investment Plans benefit from rupee-cost averaging and compounding. Starting early — even with small amounts — significantly increases your corpus over time.

Compound Interest: Albert Einstein reportedly called it the "eighth wonder of the world." More frequent compounding (monthly vs annually) noticeably increases returns over long periods.
